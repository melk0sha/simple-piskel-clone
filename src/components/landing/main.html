Piskel Clone Editor

Piskel is a free online editor for animated sprites & pixel art

This awesome application provides user-friendly functionality for drawing pixel picture with posibilities to filling closed areas, picking a color of every pixel, erasing, drawing straight lines and other amazing tools. You can add, delete and copy a frame for flexible animation here. Apart from that a ability of downloading your animation will make you enjoy.
